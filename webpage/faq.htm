General Information

The mindlytabletop.com is a online board game shop. It allows users to browse, search, as well as purchase games and find for tutorial. Our aim is to allow everyone to experience the joy of board games.

The Mindly Tabletop Cafe is physical outlets whereby a customer makes his/her purchases over the counter; whereas Mindly Tabletop Store is a virtual bookstore whereby the customer makes his/her purchases online at their own comfort.


On Products And Services


There is no price difference. However, we do run different kinds of promotion from time to time.
Our Return Policy allows for a refund only if incorrect or defective product(s) delivered. However, the policy requires that the incorrect or damaged product be returned to the following address within 7 days from the date of receipt of the book.

Tanjung Tokong, 11200 Tanjung Bungah, Penang
You can visit mindlytabletop.com to check the stock available or not. If not, you can pre-order and waiting for the stock refill.


On Orders


Step 1: Login in mindlytabletop.com
Step 2: Pick the product(s) you want to buy
Step 3: Check the carts for correct names and quantities
Step 4: Press confirm to make an order
An e-mail will be sent to you to confirm your order. Also, an Order ID will be given to you, which you can use to check your order status.
All orders must be placed on mindlytabletop.com . We do not accept any phone calling orders.
